Services

Every property is different, but most projects fall into these main buckets.

Habitat & Hunt Layouts

Stand locations, access, bedding, and food planned around wind, pressure, and how deer actually move on your ground.

  • Property walk-through and mapping
  • Entry/exit and wind-based stand placement
  • Food plot and screening recommendations

Timber Stand Improvement

Thin, mark, and release to favor the best stems while protecting soil, access, and future options.

  • On-the-ground stand assessment
  • TSI marking and priority zones
  • Coordination with loggers where needed

Land Stewardship Planning

Long-view plans that tie habitat, timber, access, and family goals into one working roadmap.

  • 3–10+ year written plan
  • Phase-by-phase project list
  • Rough budgets and timelines
