Services

Every property is different, but these are the buckets most projects fall into.

Habitat & Hunt Layouts

Stand locations, bedding areas, food, and travel routes laid out with wind, access, and pressure in mind.

  • Property walk-through and mapping
  • Entry/exit route planning
  • Recommended bedding and food plot locations

Timber Stand Improvement

Thinning and crop-tree release to improve health, growth, and long-term value of your best trees.

  • Marking and prescription recommendations
  • Coordination with loggers where needed
  • Follow-up monitoring and adjustments

Stewardship & Long-Term Plans

Multi-year plans that balance hunting, income, and conservation so your land improves over time.

  • 3–10 year written plan
  • Priority projects by phase
  • Estimated timelines and budgets
