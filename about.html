About 23 Timber Co.

23 Timber Co. exists for landowners who see their property as more than a line on a tax map. If you care about wildlife, timber, and what your land looks like in 20+ years, you’re in the right place.

We combine practical forestry, habitat design, and real hunting experience on New England ground to help you make better decisions with your acres.

What drives the work

  • Healthy forests that outlast us
  • Better habitat for deer, bear, and non-game species
  • Clear plans so owners know what to do next

How we like to work

Simple, honest, and based on what the land actually shows us.

Walk first, talk later

We start with boots on the ground, not a template. Terrain, sign, timber, and access all matter.

Practical recommendations

No fluff. Just clear steps you can act on, with realistic timelines and trade-offs.

Long-view mindset

We care about what your land looks like in 10–30 years, not just one season or one cut.
