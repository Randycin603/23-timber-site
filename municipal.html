Municipal Work

Practical forestry and habitat work for towns, conservation commissions, and public land managers in New Hampshire and northern New England.

Hazard Trees & ROW Management

Identify and prioritize risk trees along roads, trails, and recreation areas so crews can work safely and budgets go where they matter most.

Town Forest & Park Planning

Layouts for trails, access, and habitat that respect existing use, wildlife, and long-term timber value.

Storm & Resilience Projects

Assess windthrow risk, wet spots, and access issues ahead of time so the next big storm does less damage and is faster to clean up.
